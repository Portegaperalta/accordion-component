Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our frontend challenges website.

Can I user FrontendPro to prepare for a frontend job interview?

Yes, FrontendPro prepares you to develop the skills neccesary to land your first job.

Do i need to have experience in frontend dev to user FrontendPro?

FrontendPro is designed to accommodate developers of all skill levels. Our challenges range from beginner to advanced, so there's something for everyone.

How often are new challenges added to FrontEndPro?

We add new challenges every week for you to constantly improve.

What kind of frontend challenges can i expect to find on FrontendPro?

You can expect challenges of all types, from beginner, to intermediate, to advanced.

Can i use libraries/frameworks on these projects?

No, it is not practical for you to use them, you should use pure hardcode code.
